# Learner Guide

- Start at the landing page and complete the 7 assessment modules.
- **Dashboard**: progress, scores, badges. **Learning** (`/learning`): paths, modules,
  lessons, quizzes, XP, levels, streaks, certificates.
- **Simulations**: AI-generated scenarios; after each, view AI analysis and, where enabled,
  **Voice Replay** (`/learning/voice/:id`) with pace, fillers, confidence, and coaching.
- **Analytics** (`/learning/analytics`): skill radar and trends over time.
- Earn achievements and climb the leaderboard. Your trainer may assign work and leave
  feedback — acknowledge it from your notifications.
